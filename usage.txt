Usage:
  nim_norg {SUBCMD}  [sub-command options & parameters]
where {SUBCMD} is one of:
  help      print comprehensive or per-cmd help
  parse     
  generate  

nim_norg {-h|--help} or with no args at all prints this message.
nim_norg --help-syntax gives general cligen syntax help.
Run "nim_norg {help SUBCMD|SUBCMD --help}" to see help for just SUBCMD.
Run "nim_norg help" to get *comprehensive* help.
